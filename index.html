Questions

Good to know

Do you come to me?+

Yes. Marten Mobile Detailing is fully mobile. Edward comes to your home or work in Nampa and across the Treasure Valley, so there is no drop off and no waiting.

What areas do you serve?+

Nampa is home base, and Edward also details vehicles in Caldwell, Meridian, Eagle and the Boise area.

How much does a detail cost?+

Pricing depends on the size and condition of your vehicle and the service you choose. Call or text for a quick, honest quote with all the options laid out.

Do you offer recurring washes?+

Yes. Many customers set a weekly, bi-weekly or monthly schedule so their vehicle stays clean without the hassle.

Can you remove pet hair and odors?+

Yes. Pet hair removal and odor treatment are some of the most requested services, and they make a big difference for dog owners.
